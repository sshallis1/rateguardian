## REGULATORY NOTE (Trade Guardian Brief)

Publisher exclusion (Investment Advisers Act, Section 202(a)(11)(D)) covers general market commentary. You do NOT need SEC/FINRA registration for the brief as long as:
- No personalized recommendations ("Based on YOUR portfolio, buy X")
- Use "watching" / "consider" / "general observation" language
- Disclaimer on every piece of content

If you move to personalized recommendations later (Trade Guardian Co-Pilot mode), you'll need:
- Series 65 exam ($187, 2-4 weeks study)
- RIA registration ($15-25K startup, $5-15K/year)
- Compliance attorney review ($2-5K one-time)

**For now: the brief is clean. Ship it.**
